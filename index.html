About Us

Royal College of Health Technology, Iludun-Oro, Kwara State, is a forward-thinking institution dedicated to the training and development of competent healthcare professionals equipped to meet the evolving demands of the healthcare sector. Established in 2023, the College was founded with a commitment to providing quality health education, practical skills, and professional excellence in a conducive learning environment. As a privately owned institution, Royal College of Health Technology is duly approved and accredited by the appropriate regulatory authorities, ensuring that its academic programmes meet national standards and best practices in health education. The College is committed to producing highly skilled graduates who possess the knowledge, competence, and ethical values required to contribute meaningfully to healthcare delivery and community development. At Royal College of Health Technology, learning extends beyond the classroom. Through a combination of theoretical instruction, practical laboratory sessions, clinical exposure, and modern teaching methodologies, students are prepared to excel in their chosen fields and become effective healthcare practitioners. The institution places a strong emphasis on professionalism, innovation, integrity, and service to humanity.

Our Vision

To develop and improve public health services nationwide and beyond, through the pursuit of excellence in manpower training, research and effective service delivery.

Our Mission

To create, preserve and communicate knowledge in health science, health technology, behavioural and Management science. To contribute to the complete enhancement of Health and well being of Nigeria.

Available Programmes

ND/HND COMMUNITY HEALTH

Build the knowledge and practical skills required to promote primary healthcare, prevent diseases, and improve community wellbeing. Train to become dedicated to serving individuals, families, and communities.

HND PUBLIC HEALTH

Learn to address public health challenges through disease prevention, health promotion, epidemiology, and community health interventions. Prepare to make a meaningful impact on population health and development.

HEALTH TECHNICIAN

Acquire practical healthcare skills and technical knowledge necessary to support healthcare professionals in delivering quality patient care. Train for a fulfilling career in the health sector.

COMMUNITY HEALTH EXTENSION WORKER

Gain the expertise needed to promote community health, prevent diseases, and provide essential healthcare services at the grassroots level. Become a key contributor to public health and community wellbeing.

ND/HND PHARMACEUTICAL TECHNOLOGY

Develop the knowledge and practical skills required in drug production, dispensing, quality control, and pharmaceutical services. Build a rewarding career in the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries.

ND/HND HEALTH INFORMATION MANAGEMENT

Manage healthcare information effectively through training in medical records, health data systems, information technology, and healthcare administration. Prepare for a vital role in modern healthcare delivery.
