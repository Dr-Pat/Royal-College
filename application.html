Apply for Admission

Begin your journey towards a rewarding career in the healthcare profession at Royal College of Health Technology, Iludun-Oro, Kwara State. We welcome qualified candidates who are passionate about making a positive impact in the health sector through quality education, practical training, and professional development

Admission Process

Prospective applicants are required to obtain the official admission application form directly from the College campus. Application forms are available at the Admissions Office during official working hours.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the Royal College of Health Technology campus at Iludun-Oro, Oro, Kwara State.
  2. Obtain the official admission application form from the Admissions Office.
  3. Complete the form accurately and provide all required information.
  4. Attach all necessary credentials and supporting documents.
  5. Submit the completed application form to the designated office before the application deadline.

Admission Requirements

Applicants must possess the required academic qualifications for their chosen programme and provide relevant credentials during the application process. Additional requirements may apply depending on the programme of study.

Need Assistance?

For enquiries regarding admissions, programme requirements, application procedures, or other related information, kindly visit the College or contact the Admissions Office during working hours.

Take the first step towards a successful future in healthcare. We look forward to welcoming you to the Royal College of Health Technology community.
